About Me


Hi! My name is Robin Thorn but I'm better known online by the name Thawney. I like to think of myself as a jack of all trades and master of none but to try and concisely sum me up I'm a Nonbinary Musician, Programmer, Editor, Designer, Sound Technician and Creator from the UK! Whilst I may be young I have over seven years of experience in the theatre and music industries, have amassed a small following online and have achieved a few million streams on my musical projects (thanks so much to people like you who are interested in what I do).

Currently I am exploring more programming for music technology, video editing work and music production for various friends' projects.
